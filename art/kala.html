52 art forms


Kala means performing art in Sanskrit. In Hindu scripture, Shiva is the master of Kala. In the Lalita Sahasranama, the Devi is invoked as an embodiment of the 64 fine arts.

The mastery of over 64 kinds of skills is called chatushashti Kalas.
